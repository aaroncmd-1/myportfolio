Internship

IDEase

Role: Frontend Developer Intern

The problem: The existing ID creation system used by RAKSO CT had performance issues, an outdated interface, and an inefficient workflow that slowed down the ID production process.

The solution: Our team developed IDEase, a functional prototype that modernized the workflow with a cleaner interface, improved usability, and a more efficient foundation for future development.

My part: I contributed to the project from its earliest stages by helping shape the product's identity, including proposing the name IDEase and collaborating on the initial UI designs in Figma. I was also solely responsible for creating the system flowchart used during planning and development. As development progressed, I joined the frontend team, implementing the approved designs into the application. I was responsible for the frontend implementation of the Records page, working alongside a teammate who handled the backend functionality while collaborating with the team to deliver a consistent and user-friendly interface.
