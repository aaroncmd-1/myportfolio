Capstone Project

AniKwento

AI-powered storytelling platform for kindergarten education.

Role Frontend Developer & UI/UX Contributor
Timeline Capstone project, 2025–2026
Tools Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Laravel, MySQL
01

Overview

AniKwento is an AI-powered storytelling platform designed to help kindergarten teachers create engaging, personalized learning experiences. By combining generative AI, interactive storytelling, and teacher-friendly customization tools, the platform transforms traditional storytelling into a more immersive classroom activity that promotes literacy, creativity, and student participation.

02

The Problem

Young learners are increasingly drawn to digital content, making it more challenging for teachers to capture and maintain their attention through traditional storytelling methods. At the same time, creating engaging and age-appropriate lesson materials requires significant preparation time.

03

The Solution

AniKwento enables teachers to generate personalized stories using AI, customize narration and classroom activities, and deliver interactive storytelling sessions through an intuitive web application. The platform helps educators create meaningful learning experiences while reducing lesson preparation time.

04

My Role

Team Capstone Project

My primary contributions focused on frontend development, documentation, UI implementation, and design quality assurance.

  • Developed and implemented frontend interfaces for key parts of the platform.
  • Assisted in translating UI designs into responsive web pages.
  • Contributed to design quality assurance by reviewing interface consistency and usability.
  • Prepared and maintained project documentation throughout development.
  • Collaborated closely with the team during the design and development process.

Key Features

  • Offline story downloads
07

Challenges

One of the biggest challenges was designing an interface that remained simple and intuitive for teachers while supporting multiple AI-powered customization features. We also had to balance usability with the limitations of AI services and third-party APIs during development.

08

Outcome

AniKwento was successfully developed as our capstone project and demonstrated how AI can support early childhood education through personalized storytelling. The project showcased how thoughtful interface design and modern web technologies can create engaging digital learning experiences for both teachers and students.

09

What I Learned

Working on AniKwento strengthened my experience in frontend development, UI implementation, and collaborative software development. It also taught me the importance of designing for real users, maintaining interface consistency, and working effectively within a multidisciplinary team to deliver a complete product.
